Previously known as Wild Explorer.
Promenade adds fancy-looking biomes and structures to the Minecraft world generation. It aims at beautifying the process of exploring the world of Minecraft.



Promenade requires Fabric Loader, Fabric API and Dawn API
Join the Dawn Town discord for support or to chat about the mods: https://discord.gg/8ksTVJu
Want to help with the translations of our mods? Check out our Crowdin project!

Biomes:

  • Pumpkin Pastures (inspired by Minecraft: Dungeons)
  • Cherry Oak Forests
  • Taller Nether Forests
  • Tritanopian, Achromatopsian and Protanopian Galleries (Nether)
  • Dark Amaranth Forests (Ender)

Animals:

  • Ducks
